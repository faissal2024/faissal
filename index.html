My Story

2021

Find a solution

You will wonder why I am looking for a solution and the solution is why ? I did not succeed in my studies and in our society this is an important study for work so here we ask why did you choose programming?

2022

The answer

As is known, website programming is in high demand in Morocco, and this field does not often require a university degree, and this is where the dream began... I started researching and understanding this field, and I loved it, but circumstances did not help me at that time.

2023

the beginning

Luck was on my side when I had a computer, even if it was weak. I saw it as a key to my passion. I started going to cafes and places where there was free internet just to download courses on the phone and go home and watch and apply them. This was my beginning in programming several projects that I loved and gave me a desire to complete them even if the capabilities did not help.

2024

The beginning of professionalism

I started creating good projects and learning more and more every day. I learned how to create a website from scratch. I showed some of my work to people and received great support. Thank God, this motivated me more. Although I did not earn a single dollar, this beautiful dream will remain in front of me until I achieve it.

2025

More work

There are only a few days left until the new year, and I promised not to waste even a little time in them, and to develop myself more and more simply because I love my work.
